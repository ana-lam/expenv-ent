Mapping Environmental Justice in NYC is the result of Ana Lam's independent research project as part of her Post Baccalaureate Fellowship at the Barnard Digital Humanities Center.

Ana is a graduate of Barnard College, where she majored in Physics and English. During her time in undergrad, she conducted astrophysics research in gravitational waves and completed independent study on postcolonial literature. Upon completing Post Baccalaureate Fellowship in the Digital Humanities, she will transition into a software engineering role at a Brooklyn startup with an environmental and social impact focus.

Ana could not have completed this project without the support of her brilliant mentors at the DHC: Alicia Peaker, Kaiama Glover, and Miriam Neptune.
